University of Arizona

Home to the Biosphere 2 Agrivoltaic Learning Labs. Pioneering research on the food-energy-water nexus in arid lands, demonstrating how shading crops reduces water stress while improving panel efficiency.

Mexico

UNAM

Leading the "PASE" project (Parcela Agrovoltaica Sostenible y Educacional) at Topilejo. The Institute of Renewable Energies (IER) focuses on rain harvesting, optimized radiation dosing, and crop protection in high-altitude climates.

Visit IER-UNAM
LatAm

Alianza Agrovoltaica

A multinational alliance fostering cross-border collaboration between Chile, Colombia, Brazil, and beyond. Promoting regulatory frameworks and knowledge sharing to accelerate agrivoltaics adoption across the continent.

Visit Alliance
Mexico

RAME

Red de Agrivoltaica Mexicana. The national network connecting researchers, farmers, and government policymakers to create sustainable, dual-use business models specific to Mexico's diverse climatic zones.
